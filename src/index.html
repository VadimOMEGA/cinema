How can I book tickets online?

You can book tickets directly on our website by selecting your movie, showtime, and preferred seats. Payment can be made securely online, and you'll receive a digital ticket via email.

Does Capital Cinema offer discounts for students?

Yes! We offer discounted tickets for students. Just bring a valid ID when purchasing your ticket at the counter or select the discount option when booking online.

What types of movies do you screen?

We feature a mix of the latest Hollywood blockbusters, independent films, and classic screenings. Special events and themed movie nights are also available.

Can I bring outside food and drinks?

Outside food and drinks are not allowed. However, we offer a variety of snacks, popcorn, and beverages at our concession stand.
